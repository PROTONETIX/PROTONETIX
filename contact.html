Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 business hours. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our office directly at +91 7019458261 during business hours.

Do you offer product demonstrations?

Yes, we offer comprehensive demonstrations of all our products. You can request a demo through our contact form by selecting "Request a Demo" in the subject dropdown, or by emailing info@protonetix.com with your specific interests.

Can I schedule a visit to your office?

Absolutely! We welcome visitors to our Bangalore office. Please contact us in advance to schedule an appointment so we can ensure the appropriate team members are available to meet with you and address your specific interests.

How do I report a technical issue with your products?

For technical support, please email info@protonetix.com with a detailed description of the issue, including any error messages, the product version, and steps to reproduce the problem. Our technical team will assist you as quickly as possible.

Do you provide customized solutions for specific educational needs?

Yes, we specialize in tailoring our solutions to meet the specific needs of educational institutions. Please contact our team with details about your requirements, and we'll work with you to develop a customized approach that addresses your unique challenges and goals.
